About The Club?

Data Science CU is a student organization preparing students for successful careers in data science and analytics. We educate pupils the fundamentals of data science subjects such as data visualization, manipulation, predictive modelling, and machine learning. We aim to promote a learning environment for students of all levels, with a focus on R, Python and SQL. The club is accessible to all levels of study.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need Data Science experience to join the club?

    No! If you have little to no data science or coding experience, there is a beginner route for newcomers designed to teach the basics and fundamentals needed to be well on your way to becoming a better data scientist.

  • Are there requirements and strict rules within the club?

    Not at all! After all, this club is run by students! The club is designed to groom and polish your data science skills, but it is no way a stressful club to be a part of. Every member and officer is a friendly and compatible person to work with that you can form friendships with. From this club, you will get what you put in!

  • When are the meetings and how often am I supposed to attend them?

    Meetings begin every Saturday at 12pm and typically last for about two hours. Meetings will not take during exam periods, or as directed by the coordinator. You should attend whenever you can, but try to make it to every meeting. If you miss a meeting, it’s totally understandable. Just make sure to check in with your project group so you don’t feel out of the loop from week to week

  • Do I need to be a Computer Science or Statistics major to join the club?

    Absolutely not! Anybody with an interest in learning about Data Science is welcome in this club, no matter your major or intended field!

  • Which languages will I be using within the club?

    Typically, our project groups will be working predominantly in either Python or R

  • What do I need to bring to the meetings?

    Just bring your computer and your knowledge and you’ll be good to go!
